Welcome to LARQ – Hydration, Evolved

At LARQ, we believe that hydration should be more than a habit—it should be a lifestyle rooted in sustainability, innovation, and health. Our mission is to revolutionize the way the world drinks water by providing beautifully designed, self-cleaning water bottles and cutting-edge filtration systems that not only prioritize personal health but also protect the planet.

Our Story

LARQ was born out of a desire to create a simple yet impactful solution to two major problems: access to clean drinking water and the harmful effects of single-use plastics on our environment. What started as a passion project has evolved into a global movement for conscious consumers who demand more from the products they use daily. We didn't just want to build a bottle—we wanted to build a better future.

With our patented PureVis™ UV-C LED technology, our bottles neutralize up to 99.9999% of harmful bacteria and viruses. It's a smart, effective, and sustainable alternative to traditional water bottles that often harbor germs and contribute to global waste.
