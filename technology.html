Innovative Technology for Better Hydration

At LARQ, we harness the power of cutting-edge technology to deliver the purest, safest water with every sip. Learn about the advanced tech behind our self-cleaning water bottles and pitchers.

UV-C Sterilization Technology

At the heart of LARQ's products is our innovative UV-C sterilization technology. UV-C light is a form of ultraviolet light that is known to be effective at eliminating harmful bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens.

How It Works

The LARQ bottle's UV-C light system activates a powerful 60-second sterilization cycle that destroys the DNA of microorganisms, effectively neutralizing them and making your water safe to drink.

Nano Zero Filtration

LARQ products feature advanced filtration systems that remove impurities from water. The Nano Zero filtration technology uses a multi-stage filter that captures and removes chlorine, heavy metals, and other harmful substances.

Dual Protection

Combines UV-C light and Nano Zero filtration for the cleanest water possible.

Pure Taste

Removes contaminants that affect water taste and quality.

Rechargeable Battery Technology

Our products are powered by high-performance, rechargeable lithium-polymer batteries. This energy-efficient technology allows our bottles and pitchers to operate for long periods without frequent recharging.

  • Up to one month of use on a single charge
  • USB-C charging for universal compatibility
  • Eliminates need for disposable batteries

Smart Hydration Monitoring

LARQ products are designed to encourage healthy hydration habits. Our smart technology includes a hydration reminder feature that encourages you to drink water regularly.

The bottle's LED indicator lights up when it's time for a drink, ensuring you stay hydrated throughout the day.
